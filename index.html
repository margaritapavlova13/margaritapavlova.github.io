About Me

I'm an Economics PhD Candidate at CERGE-EI, Prague. My general fields of inerest are Labor Economics and Gender Economics. My research focuses on how macroeconomic conditions, labor market policies, and institutional factors shape labor market outcomes. I am particularly interested in the long-term effects of economic shocks, wage determination, and labor market inequalities.
